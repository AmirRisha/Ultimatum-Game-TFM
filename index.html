Adaptive Repeated Ultimatum Game

Instructions

You may be matched with a rule-based player that behaves in one of two consistent ways: one that prioritizes its own monetary payoff, and one that also considers fairness in the distribution of payoffs. The behavior of this player is pre-programmed and does not change during the experiment.

  1. Configure mode and treatment parameters.
  2. Play all rounds (accept/reject or propose split).
  3. Review round-by-round outcomes and download CSV logs.
